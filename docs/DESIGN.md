# Design

The interface should be a compact management cockpit, not a landing page.

## Screens

- Draft room: budget, player pool, selected five.
- Cup bracket: central non-match hub with an eight-team tree and next opponent.
- Match room: text log, current score pressure, tactical choices.
- Season summary: three cup outcomes and award snippets.
- Career summary: total championships after up to three seasons.

## Visual Tone

Use restrained esports energy:

- dark neutral base with team-accent color chips.
- clear tables for players and teams.
- readable match log typography.
- no decorative hero page before the game.
