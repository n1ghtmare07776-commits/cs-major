# v0.1 Vertical Slice

## Goal

Create the smallest playable version of CS Cup Manager.

## Scope

- Static roster content for eight teams.
- Draft five players under budget.
- Auto-fill opponent teams.
- Run one cup with eight teams.
- Resolve matches with seeded text simulation.
- Simulate the player's matches in detail and resolve non-player matches quickly through simplified AI advancement.
- Show cup champion and cup MVP summary.

## Out of Scope

- Online play.
- Local two-player mode.
- Live roster fetching.
- Full economy simulation.
- Best-of-three map veto.
- Accounts or cloud saves.

## Acceptance

- A player can complete one cup without editing data files.
- If the player is eliminated, the remaining cup resolves quickly and announces a champion.
- Every completed cup announces champion and cup MVP.
- Re-running the same seed produces the same bracket and match results.
- At least three tactical prompt types exist.
- The player's match opening always asks for economy and tactic separately.
- Non-player bracket matches resolve without full match UI.
- The final screen shows champion, cup MVP, and player team result.
